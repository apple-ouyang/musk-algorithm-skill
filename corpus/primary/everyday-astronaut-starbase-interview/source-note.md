# Source Note

## Why it matters

This is the strongest public source in the corpus for Musk's five-step engineering method. It contains the ordered list and a short explanation of each step.

## Key takeaways

- Musk explicitly frames the method as a five-step engineering process.
- He says the steps must be completed in order.
- The steps are:
  1. Make the requirements less dumb.
  2. Try very hard to delete the part or process.
  3. Simplify and optimize the design.
  4. Accelerate cycle time.
  5. Automate.
- He warns that smart engineers often optimize things that should not exist.
- He links the method to manufacturing and engineering judgment, not generic productivity advice.
